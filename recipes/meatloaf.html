Meatloaf!

This recipe has been passed down through generations by my wife's family. I hope you enjoy the savory taste of this absolutely delicious meatloaf!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Onion
  • 1 tbsp W. Sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Sage
  • 1 clove of garlic (crushed)

Instructions

  • Mix ingredients together/
  • Roast in 350F oven for 60-90 minutes
